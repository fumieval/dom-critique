Frequently asked

Questions we get a lot.
Where are the comments stored?

By default, in the visitor's localStorage under a key derived from the page path. You can override the key when you call mount().

Does it work with single-page apps?

Yes — we observe the DOM and re-resolve selectors when nodes mount or unmount. Stable IDs and data-testid attributes give the best results.

Can I theme the UI?

The widget renders inside a Shadow DOM, so it never collides with your styles. Theming hooks are on our roadmap.

How do I export comments to a PR?

Click Copy Markdown in the floating toolbar. Each comment becomes a heading with the selector, ARIA attributes, and React source location attached.
